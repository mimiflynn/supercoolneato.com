Winter is Coming

I’m back to scouring Internet Artchive for the best live shows I’ve not already discovered:

Spoon - Live at Treasure Island on 2007-09-16 is a very good binaural recording. Listen with headphones if you don’t have an ideal stereo setup.

Death Cab for Cutie Live at Street Scene - Best Buy Stage on 2005-07-30 is a very good recording especially for being at an outdoor stage.

Andrew Bird Live at Mercy Lounge on 2007-04-16 is another fantastic recording from yet another fantastic Andrew Bird show.
